Fill in the role details and the dimensions participants will allocate points across. Save to generate a shareable URL that carries the configuration.

Each participant distributes this many points across the dimensions. 20 is a good default for 4 to 6 dimensions.

Each dimension needs a short ID (1 to 2 letters, used in the copy code), a label, and a hint explaining what it means. Add between 3 and 8 dimensions.
